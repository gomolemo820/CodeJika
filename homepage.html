About CodeJIKA

Democratizing technology education across Africa through innovative, accessible coding curriculum

Our Mission

CodeJIKA is dedicated to democratizing technology education across Africa. We believe that every student deserves access to digital skills that can transform their future.

Our core mission focuses on providing critical first exposure to web development through HTML, CSS, and JavaScript using an offline-friendly, simple curriculum designed specifically for African schools.

By training teachers and mobilizing volunteers, we operate at scale to spark interest in technology and digital creation, opening pathways to potential tech careers for the next generation.

World Wide Web Foundation Partnership

As a proud initiative under the World Wide Web Foundation, CodeJIKA benefits from global expertise in web technologies while maintaining our focus on African educational needs and infrastructure realities.
